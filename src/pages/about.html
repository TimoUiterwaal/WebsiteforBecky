About This Place

The Origin Story

This started because I love two things: cats and coffee. Not in a "I have a favourite mug" way. In a "I have opinions about espresso extraction ratios and I will narrate my cat's inner monologue unprompted" kind of way.

One day I thought: what if there was a website that was just... cat and coffee games? No login. No monetisation. No dark patterns. Just vibes, some emoji, and small games you can play on your lunch break.

So I built it. This is it. You're here. Welcome.
